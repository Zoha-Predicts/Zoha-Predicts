## 💻 Projects

### 1. House Price Predictor
**Goal:** Predict house prices based on square footage

**Results:**
- R² Score: **0.951** (95.1% accuracy)
- Mean Absolute Error: **$23,917.55**
- Model: Linear Regression
- Dataset: 100 houses (80/20 train/test split)

**Key Learning:** Simple models can be very effective with clean data.

### 2. Car Price Predictor (Homework)
**Goal:** Predict car prices based on age with depreciation

**Results:**
- Successfully implemented depreciation model
- Used custom base price and depreciation rate
- Created visualization of price vs age trend
- Model accurately predicted price decline over time

**Key Learning:** Understanding domain knowledge (depreciation) improves model design.
